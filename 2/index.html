Forrest Peak Capital

Proprietary investments. Long-term perspective.

Forrest Peak Capital acquires, holds, manages and disposes of investments and other proprietary assets for its own account. We take a selective, disciplined and long-term approach to capital allocation across international markets.

We also provide financing from our own capital to selected corporate counterparties as an ancillary treasury activity.

Cayman Islands Incorporated · International Investment Focus · Proprietary Capital

§ 01Overview

What we do.

  1. Forrest Peak Capital is a privately capitalised investment company incorporated in the Cayman Islands with an international investment focus.
  2. Our principal business is the acquisition, holding, management and disposal of investments and other proprietary assets for our own account.
  3. We also undertake ancillary treasury activities, including providing financing from our own capital to selected corporate counterparties.
  4. We evaluate selected opportunities across the United States, United Kingdom, European Union, Cayman Islands, United Arab Emirates, Hong Kong and Singapore. Our geographic focus is not exclusive and may evolve as market conditions and investment opportunities develop.
  5. We invest selectively and manage capital with a focus on preservation, disciplined risk assessment and long-term value. We do not raise or manage capital for outside investors and do not provide investment management or advisory services to clients.
iProprietary capital
We deploy capital from our own balance sheet. We do not operate a fund, accept deposits or manage third-party money.
iiSelective approach
We assess each investment individually, considering its structure, underlying economics, risks, jurisdiction and long-term value.
iiiLong-term ownership
We may hold investments over extended periods and actively monitor their performance, risks and strategic development.
§ 02Investment Approach

How we allocate capital.

Our activities include acquiring and holding equity interests, debt instruments and other proprietary assets. The form, jurisdiction, structure and duration of each investment depend on its commercial characteristics, risk profile and strategic fit.

aEquity investments
Direct or indirect minority and strategic interests in selected businesses and investment structures.
bDebt instruments
Acquisition and holding of corporate debt, notes, receivables and other credit-related assets for our own account.
cOther proprietary assets
Other assets and investment opportunities that meet our internal commercial, legal, compliance and risk criteria.
dPortfolio management
Ongoing monitoring, risk assessment, restructuring, disposal and realisation of proprietary investments.
eInvestment horizon
Flexible holding periods determined by the characteristics and objectives of each investment.
fCapital source
All investments are made using Forrest Peak's own capital. We do not pool or manage money for outside investors.
gInternational scope
We consider investments and transactions across selected international jurisdictions, taking into account local legal requirements, market conditions, currency exposure, counterparty risk and cross-border structuring considerations.

§ 04Activities

Selective corporate financing.

As an ancillary part of its proprietary investment and treasury activities, Forrest Peak provides financing from its own capital to selected corporate counterparties in its geographic focus markets and, where appropriate, in other jurisdictions.

Financing transactions are considered privately and on a case-by-case basis. Each transaction is subject to the nature and structure of the opportunity, the relevant jurisdictions, satisfactory due diligence, internal approval, contractual agreement and applicable law.

Forrest Peak does not provide consumer lending, accept deposits, operate a public lending platform or offer financing services to the general public.

iCorporate counterparties only
Financing is provided only to selected corporate counterparties and is not available to individuals or retail customers.
iiProprietary capital
All financing is provided from Forrest Peak's own capital and not from customer deposits or externally managed investor funds.
iiiSelective transactions
Forrest Peak does not publish standard financing products or maintain an open application programme. Each transaction is assessed individually.
ivCross-border assessment
Transactions involving multiple jurisdictions are subject to appropriate legal, tax, compliance and regulatory review.
§ 05Governance

Disciplined oversight.

Investment and treasury decisions are subject to internal review, legal and commercial assessment, and proportionate compliance controls. We evaluate the nature of each asset, the relevant counterparties, the source and use of funds, jurisdictional considerations and the risks associated with each transaction.

iCommercial review
We assess the underlying business, asset, transaction structure, financial position and expected risk-adjusted outcome.
iiCounterparty diligence
Relevant counterparties and beneficial owners are subject to proportionate identity, ownership, sanctions and reputational checks.
iiiOngoing monitoring
We monitor material investments and exposures throughout the period in which they are held.
ivCompliance
AML, KYC, sanctions and other applicable compliance measures are applied according to the nature, jurisdiction and risk of each transaction.
