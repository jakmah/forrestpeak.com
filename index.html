Approach

We invest where the crowd has left.

Forrest Peak takes a non-correlated path: deeply researched bets in capital-starved industries, with no benchmark to hug and no quarterly drumbeat to satisfy. Time is the asset most market participants cannot allocate. We can.

Concentrated

5–12 names at full conviction. No closet indexing, no pretend-diversification of bad theses.

Patient

Holding periods measured in cycles. We underwrite to a decade, not to next month's NAV mark.

Unloved

Sectors others have abandoned: extractives in transition, brick-and-mortar consumer, niche industrials.

Strategy

What we look for in a position.

Asymmetry

3–5x upside over the holding period against capped downside via tangible book or cash on balance sheet.

Misjudged decline

Industries written off as terminal that have a decade of cash left and meaningful end-state value.

Owner-operators

Skin-in-the-game leadership. We back people who would buy more, not collect a salary and exit.
