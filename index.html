Mandate

What we do.

Forrest Peak lends its own capital to established firms with a track record. That covers trading desks as well as high-growth companies scaling real demand. We lend through a convertible loan to your operating company: senior debt now, with the option to convert into a small equity stake later. The money lands in your own treasury and you run it under your own name.

Our own money

We lend off our own balance sheet. There are no outside investors behind us, so nobody can pull the funding out from under you.

Convertible, not controlling

It begins as debt. If it ever converts, we take only a minority stake. Your control and your cap table stay where they are on day one.

Up to $10M

Each facility is sized to what the business can actually carry, not to a number we need to hit.

Structure

How a facility works.

The paperwork is ordinary lender documentation. We send the capital to your company's own bank account, never into a trading account for you. Your brokers and counterparties only ever see your money, in your name. The loan sits on your balance sheet and we are simply a creditor behind it.

Instrument
A senior convertible loan to your company, with the usual lender protections.
Use of proceeds
Trading or working capital, and growing a model that already works.
Disbursement
Paid to your company's own bank account. You fund your own trading and operating accounts from there.
Conversion
We can convert into a minority stake on agreed triggers. Until then it is just debt, and nothing on your cap table moves.
Facility size
Up to $10M, set against your real capacity and risk.
Term
Several years, matched to your strategy, with regular reporting along the way.

Process

How to apply.

Send us a short email to start. We come back quickly and ask for real paperwork early.

01 · Introduction
Email info@forrestpeak.com with the basics: the company, what your track record or operating history looks like, how the model works, how much of your own money is in it, and the facility size you want. No pitch deck.
02 · Initial review
We look at whether it fits what we do and get back to you. Most enquiries stop here.
03 · Due diligence
If it goes further, we run full diligence on the company and its founders: standing, source of funds, AML and KYC, and we check the track record independently.
04 · Terms
We put together a convertible loan against your verified numbers, with the conversion terms agreed up front.
05 · Funding & monitoring
The money goes to your company's treasury. We stay in touch with reporting and monitoring while the loan is live.
