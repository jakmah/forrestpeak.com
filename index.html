Mandate

We finance proven track record — as a lender, not a fund.

Forrest Peak is a non-bank provider of growth and liquidity capital to established trading firms. We deploy proprietary capital through convertible loans: senior financing to your operating entity, with an option to convert into a minority equity stake on agreed terms. Capital reaches your own treasury — you trade it in your own name, through your own broker and prime-brokerage relationships.

Proprietary capital

Our own balance sheet. No outside LPs, no fund-of-funds layering, no redemption pressure passed down to you.

Convertible structure

Debt first, with a defined and minority path to equity. Aligned for the long term without surrendering control of your firm.

Up to $10M

Sized per facility to verified trading capacity and balance-sheet strength — not to a marketing target.

Structure

How a facility is built.

Clean, lender-grade documentation. Capital is advanced to your corporate treasury — never deposited into a trading account on your behalf — so your broker relationships see your own capital, in your own name. The loan sits on your balance sheet; we sit behind it as a creditor.

Instrument
Senior convertible loan to your operating entity, with standard lender protections and covenants.
Use of proceeds
Trading capital, working capital, and measured growth of an already-proven strategy.
Disbursement
To your entity's own bank account. You fund your own trading and prime-brokerage accounts, in your own name.
Conversion
Optional conversion to a minority equity stake on defined triggers. Debt until then; no day-one cap-table or control change.
Facility size
Up to $10M, structured to verified capacity and risk limits.
Term
Multi-year, matched to the strategy's horizon, with ongoing reporting and risk monitoring.

Eligibility

Who we lend to.

We finance institutions, not individuals — established firms that can withstand deep scrutiny and document everything we will ask for.

Verifiable track record

A real, auditable multi-year performance history in liquid, mark-to-market markets. No incubation of unproven strategies.

Corporate substance

A properly constituted operating entity with clean regulatory standing and audited or auditable financials.

Aligned principals

Owner-operators with meaningful capital of their own at risk, who clear founder-level diligence without reservation.

Diligence

Rigorous by default. We decline more than we accept.

Deep EDD

Every counterparty undergoes enhanced due diligence: source of funds, corporate structure, regulatory standing, and trading infrastructure.

Founder scrutiny

We investigate the principals. Background, integrity, prior ventures, and reputation are examined before any capital moves.

Compliance-first

AML, KYC, and sanctions screening are non-negotiable. Ongoing monitoring runs for the life of the facility.
