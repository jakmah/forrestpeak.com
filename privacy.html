Privacy Policy

Last updated 7 June 2026

This policy explains how Forrest Peak Capital Ltd. ("Forrest Peak", "we", "us") handles personal data collected through this website and through your contact with us. We are the controller of that data.

What we collect

  • Information you send us, such as your name, company, email address and anything you include in your message.
  • Information we gather during due diligence if we take a discussion further, including identity, corporate and source-of-funds documentation.
  • Basic technical data your browser sends automatically, such as IP address, device and usage information.

How we use it

  • To respond to your enquiry and assess whether a financing relationship makes sense.
  • To carry out due diligence and to meet our anti-money-laundering, know-your-customer and sanctions obligations.
  • To operate, secure and improve the website.
  • To comply with law and to establish or defend legal claims.

Legal basis

Where data protection law such as the UK or EU GDPR applies, we rely on our legitimate interest in running and protecting our business, on taking steps at your request before entering an agreement, and on compliance with our legal obligations.

Sharing

We do not sell your data. We share it only with service providers acting on our behalf, with professional advisers, and with regulators or authorities where we are required to do so.

International transfers

We operate from the Cayman Islands and may process data in other countries. Where we transfer personal data across borders we take steps to ensure it remains protected.

Retention

We keep personal data for as long as needed for the purposes above and for any period required by law, including records we must retain for anti-money-laundering purposes.

Your rights

Depending on where you are, you may have the right to access, correct or delete your personal data, to object to or restrict its use, and to data portability. To make a request, contact us at the address below. Some data may need to be retained where the law requires it.

Cookies

This website uses only the cookies necessary for it to function. We do not use it for advertising. You can block cookies in your browser settings, though parts of the site may then not work as intended.

Security

We take reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect personal data against loss, misuse and unauthorised access.

Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. The version published here is the one that applies.

Contact

For any privacy question or request, write to info@forrestpeak.com.
